# DispatchToGo Branding Brief

Last updated: 2026-03-11

## Core Brand

- Business name: `DispatchToGo`
- Preferred tagline: `Dispatch with confidence.`

## Brand Idea

DispatchToGo should feel like the dependable layer between a property problem
and a verified outcome. The brand is not about flashy tech. It is about
clarity, speed, trust, and proof.

## Brand Personality

- dependable
- fast
- capable
- local
- practical
- organized

## Positioning Line

DispatchToGo helps hospitality and property operators route work to the right
vendors, track progress clearly, and close every job with proof.

## Recommended Brand System

### Color Palette

- `Dispatch Blue` - `#1557C8`
- `Midnight Navy` - `#0F172A`
- `Slate` - `#64748B`
- `Cloud` - `#F8FAFC`
- `Maple Red` - `#D84B3E`
- `Signal Mist` - `#E8F0FE`

Recommended usage:

- Blue is the main brand and CTA color.
- Navy carries trust and headline weight.
- Slate handles supporting UI and body text.
- Cloud is the base background.
- Maple Red is a restrained accent, not the main UI color.

### Typography

Recommended pairing:

- Headlines / wordmark: `Sora`
- Body / product UI: `Inter`

Why this pairing:

- `Sora` gives the brand a modern but distinctive voice.
- `Inter` keeps the product readable, neutral, and operational.

Alternate options:

- `Space Grotesk` + `Inter` for a bolder, more tech-forward feel
- `Plus Jakarta Sans` + `Manrope` for a softer hospitality feel

## Voice And Tone

Use language like:

- `Dispatch with confidence.`
- `From request to verified work.`
- `Qualified vendors. Clear proof.`
- `Less chasing. More visibility.`

Avoid:

- vague "AI transformation" language
- generic startup cliches
- overly patriotic Canadian visuals

## Logo Direction

Keep the truck as the primary symbol. Make the maple leaf a subtle signal, not
the star.

### Concept A: Half-Leaf Truck

Idea:

- Keep the truck silhouette.
- Replace the back corner of the truck box with a geometric half maple leaf.
- The leaf should be simplified, not detailed.

Why it works:

- instantly recognizable as a truck
- Canadian without being loud
- clean and scalable

### Concept B: Negative-Space Maple Cutout

Idea:

- Use a solid truck icon.
- Cut a small maple leaf out of the cargo box with negative space.

Why it works:

- premium
- subtle
- memorable
- strong for app icon, favicon, and wordmark lockup

### Concept C: Motion Leaf

Idea:

- Keep the truck simple.
- Add motion lines behind it that taper into half a maple leaf.

Why it works:

- more energetic
- reinforces dispatch speed
- useful for marketing visuals

### Recommendation

Primary direction:

- `Concept B: Negative-Space Maple Cutout`

Secondary / friendlier variant:

- `Concept A: Half-Leaf Truck`

## Wordmark Direction

Recommended approach:

- set `DispatchToGo` in `Sora`
- slightly tighten tracking
- use either one solid brand color or make `ToGo` the accent

Suggested lockups:

- icon left, wordmark right
- stacked version for social or badges
- icon-only version for app / favicon use
